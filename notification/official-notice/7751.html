Dear users, visitors, and partners,

We want to state this as clearly as possible: Aviorxt.aero has no connection, affiliation, or relationship whatsoever with the company "Aviotrix Aerospace Private Limited". We are two completely separate and unrelated entities.

Who We Are: Aviorxt.aero

Aviorxt.aero is the personal, non-commercial website and digital identity of an individual known as AviorXT. This site is a passion project, run and funded by one person. Our purpose is to share content related to:

We are not a company. We do not offer commercial services, we are not a registered business, and we have no organizational structure. We are, simply, a personal blog.

Based on public information, Aviotrix Aerospace Private Limited appears to be a registered private company. We have no knowledge of their operations, services, or personnel, beyond the fact that they are a separate legal entity that we are not affiliated with in any way.

The confusion seems to stem from the similar-sounding names. We are taking steps to correct this misidentification on search platforms.
