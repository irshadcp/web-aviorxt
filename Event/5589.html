Today's Good News

It's always good to start the day with a win. Here's a recent breakthrough from the world of AI:

New AI Model Helps Re-Discover "Lost" Texts

Researchers have successfully used a new generative AI model to read and translate parts of the Herculaneum scrolls, ancient papyri that were charred and carbonized by the eruption of Mount Vesuvius in 79 AD. The AI was able to detect subtle, previously invisible ink patterns on the blackened scrolls, revealing new insights into classical history.
